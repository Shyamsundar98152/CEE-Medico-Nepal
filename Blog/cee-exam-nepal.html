What is the Pass Mark for the CEE Exam in Nepal?

To qualify in the Common Entrance Examination (CEE) for medical programs in Nepal, candidates must secure at least 50% of the total marks.

The exam comprises 200 multiple-choice questions, each carrying one mark, totaling 200 marks.

  • Pass Mark: 100 out of 200 (50%)
  • Marking Scheme: +1 for each correct answer, -0.25 for each incorrect answer
  • Exam Duration: 3 hours

This negative marking system encourages accuracy and discourages random guessing.

CEE Exam Syllabus Overview

The CEE syllabus is based on the National Education Board (NEB) curriculum for Grades 11 and 12. It encompasses the following subjects:

  • Physics – 50 questions
  • Chemistry – 50 questions
  • Biology – 80 questions (40 Botany + 40 Zoology)
  • Mental Agility Test (MAT) – 20 questions

Total: 200 questions, 200 marks.
