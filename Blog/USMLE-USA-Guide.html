The Three Phases of USMLE Success

1

USMLE Step 1: Foundational Sciences

Focus on First Aid, Pathoma, and dedicated UWorld question blocks. Since this is now Pass/Fail, focus on mastery of high-yield concepts like pathology, pharmacology, and microbiology.

  • High-Yield Resources: First Aid & UWorld
  • Key Focus: Basic Science & Pathology
2

USMLE Step 2 CK: Clinical Knowledge

This is the current score driver for residency matching. Dedicate time to Internal Medicine, Surgery, and OB/GYN questions. Use AMBOSS or additional UWorld passes.

  • High-Yield Resources: UWorld (2nd Pass) & Clinical Guidelines
  • Key Focus: Diagnosis & Management
3

Residency Match: ERAS & Interviews

Crucial for IMGs. Focus on securing U.S. Clinical Experience (USCE), crafting a strong Personal Statement, and preparing for virtual and in-person interviews.

  • High-Yield Resources: ERAS Application & USCE Programs
  • Key Focus: Networking & Communication
