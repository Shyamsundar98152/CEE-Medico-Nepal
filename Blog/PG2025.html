MECEE-PG 2025: Notice for Foreign Candidates

1. Application Overview

Programs Offered: MD/MS, MDS, MPH, MD Ayurveda, Nursing/Midwifery, MPharm, MSc MIT, MSc Microbiology/Biochemistry, M Optometry, MPhil Clinical Psychology, MPT.

Eligibility:

  • Sub-category I (Foreign to appear): Must take MECEE-PG 2025 exam.
  • Sub-category II (Foreign NOT to appear): Apply based on prior merit (≥50th percentile in home country's equivalent exam).

Application Period:

  • Regular: 2–22 Dec 2024 (fee: NPR 8,000).
  • Late: 22–29 Dec 2024 (double fee).

2. Examination Schedule

Program GroupDate & Time (NPT)
MD/MS/NBMS, MD Ayurveda8 Feb 2025, 11 AM–2 PM
Nursing/Midwifery, MPH, MPhil9 Feb 2025, 11 AM–2 PM
MDS, MPharm, MSc MIT, MPT, etc.10 Feb 2025, 11 AM–2 PM

3. Eligibility Criteria

  • MD/MS/MDS/MPH: Relevant bachelor's degree (MBBS/BDS/BPH, etc.) + council registration.
  • Nursing/Midwifery: BN/BNS/BSc Nursing + registration.
  • MPharm/MSc MIT: Bachelor's in respective field + registration.

4. Selection Process

  • Merit-Based: Open-house matching for seats.
    • Priority to Sub-category I candidates.
    • Unfilled seats allocated to Sub-category II.
  • Documents Required: Passport, academic transcripts, council registration, photo/signature.

5. Examination Format

  • 200 MCQs (Single Best Answer).
  • Duration: 3 hours.
  • Marking: +1 for correct, −0.25 for wrong.
  • Pass Threshold: 50th percentile.

6. Admit Card & Exam Rules

  • Download: 5 days before exam via MEC portal.
  • ID Required: Passport/E-Aadhaar + printed admit card.
  • Prohibited Items: Stationery, electronics, jewelry.

7. Important Notes

  • Original documents must be submitted during admission.
  • No private practice during residency.
  • Biometric verification at exam center and admission.
