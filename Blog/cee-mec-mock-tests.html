CEE Exam Pattern & Marking Scheme

  • Format: Objective, single-correct MCQs
  • Scoring: +1 for correct, −0.25 for incorrect, 0 for unattempted
  • Timing: One sitting, fixed duration
  • Medium: English

Always verify the year’s official notice from MEC for any updates.

CEE FAQs

How should I divide time between PCB and MAT?

Follow a 70:30 split initially (PCB:MAT). Increase MAT practice in the last 3 weeks with daily 30-minute reasoning drills.

What is a good CEE mock strategy?

Warm up with 10 easy MCQs, then take a timed full mock. Post-test, classify errors: concept, calculation, or speed. Revise only the root causes within 24 hours.

Are there negative marks in CEE?

Yes. Typically −0.25 per wrong answer. Skip if you cannot eliminate to 2 choices.
