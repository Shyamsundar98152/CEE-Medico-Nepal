About CEE Medico Nepal

CEE Medico Nepal is Nepal's premier online platform dedicated to medical entrance exam preparation. We specialize in helping students excel in the Common Entrance Examination (CEE) for MBBS/BDS, as well as other medical entrance tests in Nepal and abroad.

Our platform offers:

  • Comprehensive subject-wise MCQs (Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology)
  • Structured practice tests aligned with the latest CEE syllabus
  • Detailed explanations and conceptual learning materials
  • Performance analytics to track your progress
  • Regularly updated question bank with new patterns

Founded by a team of medical professionals and educators, CEE Medico Nepal bridges the gap between classroom learning and competitive exam requirements. Our content is carefully curated by experts who understand the Nepalese medical education landscape.

Whether you're aiming for top medical colleges in Nepal or preparing for international exams, our resources are designed to give you the competitive edge you need.

Your trusted partner for medical entrance exam preparation in Nepal. We provide high-quality resources to help students achieve their dreams of becoming doctors.
