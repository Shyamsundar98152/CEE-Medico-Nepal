USMLE Step 1 Exam Overview

The USMLE Step 1 exam is a **computer-based test** that evaluates a student’s understanding of basic sciences. It includes questions from:

  • Anatomy
  • Physiology
  • Biochemistry
  • Pathology
  • Pharmacology
  • Microbiology
  • Behavioral sciences

Step 1 is considered the foundation of USMLE because it measures your ability to apply basic science knowledge to medical practice. High scores on Step 1 are especially important for **IMGs** and **MBBS graduates from India, Nepal, and Asia** aiming for competitive residencies in the USA.
