Complete Guide to the MEC Bachelor Common Entrance Examination 2026 (Revised Syllabus)

Introduction

The Medical Education Commission (MEC) of Nepal has revised the Bachelor Level Common Entrance Examination (CEE) syllabus for 2026. This examination serves as the gateway for students aspiring to pursue medical, dental, nursing, pharmacy, public health, and allied health science programs across Nepal.

The updated syllabus emphasizes a strong foundation in Biology, Chemistry, and Physics while incorporating modern concepts relevant to medical education and healthcare sciences.

In this comprehensive guide, we explore the major topics covered in the revised syllabus and provide insights into how students can prepare effectively.

Biology Section (PCB)

Biology remains one of the most important subjects for medical entrance preparation. The syllabus is divided into Zoology and Botany.

Zoology

Evolutionary Biology

Students must understand the origin of life theories, including:

  • Oparin-Haldane Theory

  • Miller-Urey Experiment

  • Lamarckism

  • Darwinism

  • Neo-Darwinism

Human evolution from Ramapithecus to modern humans is also an important area.

Animal Diversity and Classification

This unit focuses on classification and diagnostic characteristics of organisms from Protozoa to Chordata.

Animal Tissues and Histology

Topics include:

  • Epithelial tissues

  • Connective tissues

  • Muscular tissues

  • Nervous tissues

Students should understand structure, function, and location.

Study of Selected Animals

Detailed study of:

  • Plasmodium

  • Earthworm (Pheretima)

  • Frog (Rana)

Their morphology, life cycle, physiology, and significance are emphasized.

Human Biology and Physiology

One of the highest-weighted areas, covering:

  • Digestive system

  • Respiratory system

  • Circulatory system

  • Excretory system

  • Nervous system

  • Endocrine system

  • Reproductive system

Understanding organ functions and disease concepts is crucial.

Immunology and Microbial Diseases

Students must study diseases such as:

  • Tuberculosis

  • HIV/AIDS

  • Cholera

  • Typhoid

  • Influenza

  • Hepatitis

The concepts of immunity, vaccines, antibodies, and antigens are equally important.

Medical Technology and Applied Biology

Modern medical applications include:

  • Organ transplantation

  • IVF

  • Amniocentesis

  • Transgenic animals

Applied microbiology topics focus on public health and biotechnology.

Environment and Conservation

This section introduces:

  • Animal behavior

  • Environmental pollution

  • Biodiversity conservation

  • Ramsar sites

  • IUCN categories

  • Endangered species of Nepal

Botany

Basic Components of Life

Students must understand the structure and biological importance of:

  • Carbohydrates

  • Proteins

  • Enzymes

  • Lipids

  • Minerals

Biodiversity

The syllabus covers:

  • Two-Kingdom System

  • Five-Kingdom System

  • Three-Domain System

Detailed study of:

  • Bacteria

  • Viruses

  • Fungi

  • Lichens

  • Algae

  • Bryophytes

  • Pteridophytes

  • Gymnosperms

  • Angiosperms

Medicinal plants of Nepal such as Neem, Tulasi, Ginger, Sarpagandha, and Yarsagumba are also included.

Ecology and Vegetation

Important topics include:

  • Ecosystems

  • Carbon cycle

  • Nitrogen cycle

  • Climate change

  • Greenhouse effect

  • Forest types of Nepal

  • Ecological succession

Cell Biology

Students should understand:

  • Cell structure

  • Cell organelles

  • Cell division

  • Mitosis

  • Meiosis

Genetics

Major concepts include:

  • DNA and RNA

  • Central dogma

  • Mendelian genetics

  • Linkage and crossing over

  • Sex-linked inheritance

  • Genetic disorders

  • Mutation and polyploidy

Plant Physiology

Critical topics include:

  • Water relations

  • Photosynthesis

  • Respiration

  • Plant hormones

  • Seed germination

Preparation Strategy for MEC CEE 2026

To perform well in the entrance examination:

  1. Complete the syllabus systematically.

  2. Prioritize Human Physiology, Genetics, Organic Chemistry, and Modern Physics.

  3. Practice numerical problems daily.

  4. Solve previous entrance examination questions.

  5. Take regular mock tests.

  6. Revise frequently using concise notes.

Final Thoughts

The MEC Bachelor Level Common Entrance Examination 2026 syllabus is comprehensive and designed to assess students' readiness for health science education. Success requires a balanced understanding of Biology, Chemistry, and Physics along with consistent practice and revision.

Students who build strong conceptual foundations and follow a structured preparation strategy will be well-positioned to achieve excellent results and secure admission into their desired medical or health science programs.

Start early, stay consistent, and approach your preparation with confidence.
