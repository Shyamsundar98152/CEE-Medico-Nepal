About the USMLE Step 1 Exam

The United States Medical Licensing Examination (USMLE) Step 1 is a computer-based test designed to assess the basic sciences knowledge required for practicing medicine in the USA. It includes multiple-choice questions covering anatomy, physiology, biochemistry, pathology, pharmacology, microbiology, and behavioral sciences. Step 1 is often considered the foundation for residency applications, making practice with reliable mock exams essential for success.
