Your Complete Guide to the
CEE UG Form in Nepal

Essential information for students applying for the Medical & Health Science entrance exam for the 2025/2026 Academic Session.

What is the CEE UG Form?

The CEE UG Form is the official online application for the Common Entrance Examination (CEE) for the Bachelor Level (MECEE-BL 2026), conducted by the Medical Education Commission (MEC), Government of Nepal. It's the mandatory first step for students who want to pursue undergraduate health science programs like MBBS, BDS, and BSc Nursing.

  • The online application for foreign candidates is open from August 18, 2025, to September 7, 2025 (with a double fee period until September 14, 2025).
  • The form is accessible on the official MEC portal: https://entrance.mec.gov.np/.
  • A successful submission of this form is required to receive an admit card and sit for the entrance exam.

Who Can Apply?

Eligibility requirements are a minimum of 50% aggregate or a CGPA of 2.4 in core science subjects for most programs.

  • Applicants must have passed +2 / Grade 12 or an equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (PCB) as core subjects.
  • A minimum aggregate of 50% marks (or equivalent CGPA of 2.4) in the PCB subjects is required.
  • The application is open to both Nepali and international students.

Key Details in the Form

When filling out the CEE UG form, applicants are required to provide the following information and documents:

  • Personal Information: Name, Date of Birth, Nationality, etc.
  • Academic Details: Results from SEE/SLC, +2, A-levels, or equivalent examinations.
  • Document Uploads: Scanned copies of your citizenship/passport, academic transcripts, a recent passport-sized photo, and your signature.
  • Payment Details: A scanned copy of the payment voucher/slip.

Application Fee & Steps to Fill

The fee for foreign candidates is NPR 8,000 (excluding bank charges). The fee for Nepali students is NPR 4,000.

Follow these steps carefully to ensure a successful application:

  1. Visit the official MEC website at https://entrance.mec.gov.np/.
  2. Create an account using a valid email and mobile number.
  3. Pay the application fee via online banking or services like eSewa, Khalti, or direct deposit at Himalayan Bank.
  4. Fill in all personal and academic information accurately.
  5. Upload all required scanned documents and the fee payment slip.
  6. Choose your preferred exam center.
  7. Submit the form and download/print the confirmation slip for your records.

The CEE Exam Pattern

The CEE exam is a crucial step. Here are the key details about its structure.

  • Total Questions: 200 Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs)
  • Duration: 3 hours
  • Total Marks: 200
  • Pass Marks: 50%
  • Question Type: Single Best Response with four options.
  • Marking Scheme: 1 mark for a correct answer. There is **no negative marking**.

After Submission

Submitting the form is just the beginning. The next steps are crucial for your admission journey.

  • Admit Card: MEC will issue a downloadable admit card a few days before the exam.
  • The Exam: The CEE exam will be held from November 1st to November 5th, 2025, for various programs.
  • Merit List & Counseling: Based on your score, MEC will publish a merit list and conduct a matching/counseling process to allocate seats in government, private, and affiliated colleges.

In Summary

The CEE UG Form is the critical first step for any student aiming to study medicine or allied health sciences in Nepal. Filling it out correctly and on time is non-negotiable for anyone who wishes to sit for the entrance exam and apply for admission.
