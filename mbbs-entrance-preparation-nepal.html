MBBS Entrance Preparation in Nepal

The Common Entrance Examination (CEE) is the main gateway for studying MBBS in Nepal. Students must prepare Physics, Chemistry, Biology and MAT.

Preparation Strategy

Success in CEE requires a balanced approach between theoretical understanding and rigorous MCQ practice. Here is our recommended roadmap:

  • Complete NEB Syllabus thoroughly
  • Practice MCQ daily (Subject-wise)
  • Solve CEE & IOM Past Questions
  • Take Weekly Mock Tests
