What is CEE Nepal? Complete Overview for 2026

The CEE (Combined Entrance Examination) is administered by the Medical Education Commission of Nepal and is the single gateway exam for admission to:

  • MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery)
  • BDS (Bachelor of Dental Surgery)
  • BSc Nursing and other health science programs
  • Government and private medical colleges across Nepal

The exam tests candidates on Biology (Zoology & Botany), Chemistry, and Physics — subjects covered in the +2 (Grade 11–12) science curriculum. The CEE exam is 200 MCQs in 3 hours, with 1 mark per correct answer and negative marking for wrong answers.

CEE Biology — High-Yield Topics

Biology carries the highest weightage in CEE Nepal. Key high-yield topics include: Cell Biology and Cell Division, Genetics and Heredity, Ecology and Environment, Evolutionary Biology, Plant Physiology (Botany), Animal Physiology (Zoology), Human Anatomy and Physiology, Microbiology, and Biotechnology.

CEE Chemistry — Most Important Topics

For CEE Chemistry 2026, focus on: Chemical Bonding and Molecular Structure, Organic Chemistry (Hydrocarbons, Functional Groups), Chemical Kinetics and Thermodynamics, Electrochemistry, Coordination Chemistry, Acids, Bases and Salts, and Periodic Table properties.

CEE Physics — Core Areas for 2026

CEE Physics questions frequently come from: Mechanics (Laws of Motion, Work-Energy), Waves and Optics, Thermodynamics, Electricity and Magnetism, Modern Physics (Photoelectric Effect, Nuclear Physics), and Fluid Dynamics.
